1. Introduction

Catharsis Music Player is a free, offline, and ad-free audio player. We do not collect or process any personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

3. Local Access to Media Files

The App requests permission to access audio files stored on your device for the purpose of scanning and playing them. We do not upload or share these files.

4. No Internet Connectivity

The App works entirely offline. It does not send or receive any data over the internet.

5. No Ads or Third-Party Services

The App contains no advertisements, no trackers, and no third-party analytics tools.
